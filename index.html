Description

"LITTLE QUEST" is a pixel 2D game made for Multidisciplinary Project by GROUP-11. It is a simple one-player game that tells adventures of characters fighting enemies and bosses in a fantasy world full of different difficulties and challenges. Join us in this exciting experience, and we hope you have an enjoyable journey through the game world.

How to Play

In this 2D pixel art adventure, your ultimate goal is to collect souls, defeat fierce enemies, and battle through multiple challenging levels to confront powerful bosses. Prove your strength and rise to become the new king!
Controls & Gameplay:
• Jump with 'Space' (keyboard) or '✕' (controller). Press twice for a double jump!
• Attack using 'K' or '□'. Combine with neutral, up, or down directions for different strikes. The downward attack works only in the air.
• Heal with 'R' or '△'. You have limited charges—use them wisely.
• Interact with objects using 'E' or '◯'.
• Dash using 'Shift/L' or 'R2' to quickly avoid danger or cross gaps.
• Use the downward air attack to parry spikes and bounce upward!
• Dashing grants brief invincibility, perfect for escaping tight spots.
• Stay alert—some enemies will detect you if you get too close!
• Reaching checkpoints restores your healing charges.
• Survive and reach the end of the level to claim victory and move one step closer to the throne!
